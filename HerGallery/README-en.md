## Why We Started

Some voices disappear in an instant.
A social media post, a testimony, a screenshot —
may be here today, gone tomorrow, as if it never existed.

What we want to do is simple, yet stubborn:

**1. Preserve online posts.** In an environment where platforms can delete content anytime, we put evidence on-chain and invite everyone to be witnesses and supporters.

**2. Share rights protection experiences.** So everyone can see and learn from sisters' struggles, cheering for her actions online.

**3. HerStory.** The reconstruction and imagination of women's history, boldly rewriting women's history with an "unlimited" attitude.

**4. Women's creations.** Let women creators permanently inscribe their work on WEB3.

**5. Little moments of life.** Life is contemporary history — we encourage women to record and share more.

HerGallery is a women-themed, permanent on-chain preservation and curation platform.
Here you can record a silenced history, or showcase excellent women's creations.
We believe: **Every her story, every her voice, deserves to be remembered forever.**

All content is permanently on-chain, impossible to delete or tamper with.

**Visit online**: https://mumulin-all.github.io/HerGallery/

## Tip Mechanism

HerGallery is a non-profit community project, and the platform's continued operation depends on community support.

We accept small tips from users (in AVAX), which will be used for:
- Server and IPFS storage costs
- Contract gas fee subsidies
- Community activities and operations

Tipping is completely voluntary and does not affect the use of any features. We believe: **Every act of kindness allows this cloud to float further.**

## How Everyone Can Participate

- **Create an exhibition hall**: For an event, a woman, a kind of creation — create a permanent space. Stake 0.001 AVAX as your on-chain commitment.
- **Submit evidence**: Upload images and text, let what really happened be remembered forever.
- **Create inspired works**: Express your understanding of women's issues through words and images — this is also an exhibition hall for excellent feminist works.
- **Recommend**: Support content you identify with, let truly valuable evidence and creations be seen by more people.
- **Witness**: Say "I know this happened" — every witness is a piece of strength.
- **Tip**: Support exhibition halls you identify with, give curators motivation to keep operating.

## How It Works
Curator initiates exhibition hall → Stake 0.001 AVAX as commitment
↓
Submitter submits evidence or creations → Content permanently stored on-chain, waiting for review
↓
Curator reviews → Approved content displayed on frontend, rejected content retained on-chain but not public
↓
Everyone recommends, witnesses, tips → Exhibition hall keeps growing

## Product Position

HerGallery records public events and content that has been publicly released, and does not accept exposure of private individuals without consent.

We never delete any on-chain records. Deletion is never the right answer.
Curators have frontend display rights, but destroying the archive — no one can do that.

**The chain is the archive, the frontend is the exhibition hall.**
